Design Thinking for Everyday Problems

David Park • March 18, 2026

Design thinking isn't just for designers. It's a problem-solving approach that anyone can use to tackle challenges creatively and effectively.

At its core, design thinking is about empathy, experimentation, and iteration. It's about understanding the real problem, exploring multiple solutions, and being willing to fail and try again.

The Design Thinking Process

  • Empathize: Understand the problem from different perspectives
  • Define: Clearly articulate the challenge you're solving
  • Ideate: Generate multiple possible solutions
  • Prototype: Create quick, low-cost versions to test
  • Test: Gather feedback and refine

Whether you're planning a family vacation, organizing your home, or solving a work challenge, design thinking can help you approach it more creatively and find better solutions.

Start small. Pick one problem this week and walk through the process. You might be surprised at what you discover.
